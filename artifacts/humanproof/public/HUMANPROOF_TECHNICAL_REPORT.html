Executive Overview

HumanProof is a 5-dimension weighted risk assessment platform that calculates AI displacement risk for any job, skill, or career portfolio. It combines:

  • 228 verified skills with 2026-calibrated risk scores

Core Value Proposition: Not just "your job is at risk" — but "here's exactly what to do about it"

Key Algorithms

Custom Skill Risk Scoring (3-Layer)

Layer 1: Check for protective skill patterns
  → 'AI ethics', 'governance', 'oversight' = 8-15 (safe)

Layer 2: Check for high-automation patterns
  → 'data entry', 'form filling', 'scheduling' = 82-92 (risky)

Layer 3: Keyword matching
  → human keywords: empathy, leadership, crisis, mentor
  → tech keywords: code, automate, generate, analyze
  → baseScore = (techCount / total) * 82 + (humanCount / total) * 10

Layer 4: Industry modifier
  → Finance 1.35x | Tech 1.25x | Healthcare 0.65x
  → finalScore = Math.min(92, Math.max(8, baseScore * multiplier))
